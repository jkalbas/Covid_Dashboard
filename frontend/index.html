Our COVID-19 Info Dashboard provides comprehensive, accessible visuals to help you understand the spread of COVID-19 in your community. Visit the links above to see up-to-date graphs of COVID-19 cases/deaths by state and county and COVID-19 deaths for each age group. Our database is updated daily with new statistics from the CDC. For more information, visit the CDC website.

COVID-19 causes a variety of symptoms such as fever, chills, cough, shortness of breath, fatigue, and loss of taste/smell. Since March 2020, the World Health Organization has declared COVID-19 a pandemic and its presence remains worldwide. You can protect yourself by wearing a face mask in public and getting vaccinated.
